The Ohla Burrito

The ohla burrito is a delicious creation by Ohla Mexican Kitchen. Combing the delocious flavors of mexican styled meats with the freshest ingredients possible. The ohla burrito is a must eat when in taipei

Please vist our restaurant in zhongshan district of taipei city. We look foward to seeing you!

ingredients

  • Chicken, Beef, or Pork
  • Cilantro Lime Rice, Pico de Gallo, Corn Salsa, and Lettuce
  • Fresh Tortillas

Steps

  1. Cook meat while warming tortilla
  2. Lay tortilla on foil and add corn, salsa, lettuce and rice in rows
  3. Add meat in a row
  4. Fold burrito
  5. Enjoy!
